Previous Seson: Infinite Recharge!

Last year's game was called Infinite Recharge, and it centered around scoring power cells into either a high or a low goal. However, due to Covid-19, in-person events were cancelled for 2019 and 2020. In 2020, FIRST decided to hold competition virtually. Teams would record thier robots completing several challenges, and submit the recordings to be judged. There were also several other portions of the competition that we competed in, such as the game design challenge and the chairman's award.
